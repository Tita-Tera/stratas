Why STRATAS

Support STRATAS in doing whatever it takes to support, save and protect the less privileged in our society.

Your monthly or one-time donation puts you in good company helps STRATAS target:

  • Youths,
  • Orphans,
  • School dropouts,
  • People living with HIV/AIDS,
  • Street children (homeless children),
  • Aging persons looking after orphans, and
  • Vulnerable children (O.V.C.)
