  • AREAS OF INTERVENTION

To promote and maintain health by promoting community health action for healthy living with focus on the preventioin of communicable diseases, especially HIV/AIDS, Malaria, TB, and STDS.

To promote social well-being by narrowing the gap between the advantaged and disadvantaged. Fight against social exclusion, gender discrimination, adolescent crisis and deviation.

To stimulate and promote the development of surviving skills among vulnerable groups as a means of fighting misery and poverty.

To equip the youths with life skills, and encourage creative thinking, a focus and productive lifestyle. Curb unwanted teenage pregnancy, drug and alcohol abuse.

To provide economic and health needs of disadvantaged older people through income generating projects and home based care.

That youths and other vulnerable groups are not only adequately sensitized, but are also equipped with skills that will enable them put acquired knowledge into practice.
