Why STRATAS

We are based in the North West Region of Cameroon, working in the North West and South West Region of Cameroon. We are aware of national and global challenges which motivate us to think nationally and globally while acting locally, in this way our local actions forms a synergy with the national and global efforts towards the eradication of diseases. It is by acting locally that we contribute to the Global fight against TB and HIV, Malaria Covid 19 among others. We are motivated to be part of this global noble fight. That is why we find it daunting on us to explore ways and means to overcome some of the many huddles and challenges encountered by patients, care givers and other stake holders in the fight against TB, HIV, Malaria and Covid 19.
